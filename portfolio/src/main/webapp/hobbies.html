Jerry's Hobbies

Hobbies

One thing that I enjoy to do the most is golfing in my free time. Growing up, my father was an avid golfer, and he would bring me to the range to practice once in a while. However, joining the golf team in high school was when I really started to love golf. During times when I am stressed or tense, I'd be able to walk onto the golf course to relax and find some serenity. Even in college, I would go to the course once in a while with friends to mess around. And even in quarantine, I would go to my friend's house to play golf on his golf simulator.

Another thing that I spend a lot of time doing is watching YouTube. It is scary to me how good the recommendation algorithm is on YouTube because I cannot seem to resist the content presented to me. I don't have a very particular type of videos that I watch, but if you look through my watch history, there will be fishing videos, political commentary videos, and even some episodes of Family Guy. It was quite funny to me that I was originally supposed to work on a load test for YouTube over the summer. My rationale was: if I already spend this much time on it, I might as well contribute to it myself.
