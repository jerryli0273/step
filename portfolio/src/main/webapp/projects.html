Jerry's Projects

Projects

The very first project that I worked on was a research project in high school with Professor Kaplinghat at UCI. With a team of 4, I analyzed data from the SPARC galaxy database to compare the expected values of rotational velocity of stars versus their actual velocities. The discrepancy between the two values originated from the existence of dark matter. Using all the data that we could get from the database, I modeled the distribution of dark matter in various galaxies.
