Background

In middle school, I moved back to Irvine to continue school. I really enjoyed getting to experience both cultures first-hand as I believe I have gained perspective from this transition.

In the past, I've interned for both tech roles and finance roles, and I am currently trying to figure out a solid career path to me to truly enjoy what I do daily. In working for a startup called Obviously AI as an AI mining intern, I felt immense gratification seeing the python scripts be used to guage the performance of the company. And as a consultant for Alipay, it was eye-opening to see the immediate effects of planning a successful promotional event. In this STEP internship I hope to immerse myself in the tech space and see what being a full-time SWE would entail.
